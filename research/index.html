Research

I study the interstellar medium (ISM) and the birthplaces of stars, known as HII regions. I am particularly interested in how optical emission from HII regions can be used to trace the physical conditions of the ISM, and how this informs the chemical enrichment and evolution of galaxies, using integral field spectroscopic data. I have also studied the angular momentum in jets of radio galaxies, and galactic outflows in high-redshift analog local galaxies with the DUVET survey.
